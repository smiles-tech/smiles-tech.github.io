About Us

Who is Antech Construction

Antech Construction Company Limited, established in Ghana in 2019, is a licensed and reputable construction firm certified by the Ministry of Roads and Highways. Holding a Class A2 rating for roads, airports, and related structures, along with a Class B2 rating for concrete bridges and culverts, we specialize in labour-based road works, steel structures, asphalt pavement maintenance, and load furniture.

Beyond infrastructure, we excel in residential, commercial, and industrial construction, delivering high-quality homes, office buildings, warehouses, and more. With top-tier certifications, expertise, and a commitment to excellence, Antech Construction is your trusted partner for safe, reliable, and high-standard projects.
